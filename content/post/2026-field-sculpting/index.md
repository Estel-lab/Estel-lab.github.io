Our paper **"Electric field sculpting in ultra-low noise HgCdTe avalanche photodiodes"** has been published in *Infrared Physics & Technology* (Vol. 156, 2026).

This work introduces the **Electric Field Sculpting** design strategy for HgCdTe APDs:

- Quantifies the impact of doping differentials across independent functional zones (p, i, n⁺)
- Reveals how unregulated doping discrepancies induce catastrophic polar optical phonon emission
- Demonstrates that smoothly sculpted electrostatic gradients suppress statistical gain variance
- Architects an optimally sculpted structure with full depletion and gentle electrostatic gradients
