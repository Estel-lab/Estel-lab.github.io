Our paper **"Mesoscopic behavior of single-carrier avalanche in narrow-bandgap semiconductors"** has been published in *IEEE Transactions on Electron Devices* (Vol. 73, No. 4, 2026).

This work presents a **micro–mesoscopic coupled Monte Carlo framework** to analyze avalanche chain dynamics at the critical mesoscopic scale in HgCdTe avalanche photodiodes. Key findings:

- Avalanche statistics are governed by a coupled 2-D parameter space (spatial standard deviation σ and mean free path ℓ)
- The macroscopic signal-to-noise ratio results from complex competition among multiple noise mechanisms
- An **acceleration meso-lattice (AML) structure** is proposed to engineer the mesoscopic state near the optimal operating point

This research shifts the design paradigm from macroscopic geometric optimization to direct mesoscopic dynamics regulation.
