I have started my PhD in Microelectronics at the **Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)**, supervised by Prof. Weida Hu.

My research will focus on:

- Developing mesoscopic Monte Carlo simulation frameworks for narrow-bandgap semiconductor avalanche processes
- Investigating electric field sculpting strategies for ultra-low noise HgCdTe avalanche photodiodes
- Understanding carrier transport dynamics and excess noise mechanisms in infrared APDs

Excited for this new chapter at HKUST(GZ)!
